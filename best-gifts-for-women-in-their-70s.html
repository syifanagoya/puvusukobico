Shopping for a gift for a woman in her golden years? The main priority is making sure your gift is both useful and thoughtful. If you’re shopping for a birthday or holiday gift for a woman in her 70s, whether it’s a parent, grandparent or colleague, you want to make sure you’re buying something she will actually enjoy! Forbes published a guide to shopping habits, noting “boomers account for more than 51% of the spending in the United States.” With that in mind, it may be difficult to narrow down your search, but that’s where we come in.

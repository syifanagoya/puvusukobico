Kendall Mays Age

How old is Adam Conley’s wife Kendall Mays? She is 34 years old; born in September 1984.

Kendall Mays Family

Who is the family of Kendall Mays? Apart from her new nuclear family with Adam Conley, Kendall has a mother and father named Heather L. Green and John Mays III respectively. She also had one older brother – Jacob – and three younger sisters; Morgan, Shelby and Elizabeth.

Early Life

Bolton was born on February 26, 1953, in New Haven, Connecticut, which makes him a born-in-USA American. However, his parents George Bolotin and Helen, were Russian Jewish who had emigrated from Europe and England during the world wars.

Michael’s surname was Bolotin, which he changed to Bolton when he entered show business. He was the youngest child in the family and had an elder brother Orrin and an elder sister Sandra. His family was not too well-off, and the siblings struggled for a good life early in their childhood.

Career

Bolton’s musical career began in 1975 when he cut his not so successful his first album. He had his band Blackjack, which was into hard rock and heavy metal. As a songwriter, he wrote for Laura Branigan, which was a smash hit at the US charts in 1983, and this opened the door of fortune for him. The success was repeated in 1985. Bolton also wrote for Cher and other top music stars of the time.

He had a successful career throughout the 1980s and 1990s. His 1987 hit was on Otis Redding classic song. His next hit was Georgia On My Mind. His 1989 single, How Am I Supposed To Live Without You from his album Souls Provider, fetched him his first Grammy. His 1991 album Time, Love and Tenderness won him a Grammy Award. This was for When A Man Loves A Woman.

He had a great hit in 1997, Go The Distance, which was used in the Disney film Hercules. He also had a great tour in 2001 across countries, and then also in 2007.

Bolton has performed and collaborated with several singers. In 2011 he did Jack Sparrow on stage dressed as the Captain from the pirate movie; and even collaborated with A R Rahman, the Indian music maestro.

During 2013-14, he not only came out with an album Ain’t No Mountain High Enough, but also worked on Honda ads. He gave voice to John Oliver’s Last Week Tonight episodes in 2015.

He has also acted in cameo roles playing himself in films and TV series, which includes Fox TVs Celebrity Duets in 2006. In 2010, he was in the Dancing with the Stars show. He also did a Pizza Hut ad in 2015.
